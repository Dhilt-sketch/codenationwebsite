MANUP - Home

MANUP is a non profit website set up with the sole purpose of helping men better their overall wellbeing and mental health. If you are someone that needs a little push in the right direction or you are experiencing a difficult moment in your life and you need access to some help, MANUP is there for you.

Here you will find useful links to various resources to help you through whatever journey you are on. It is a common reaction in life that men are told to "Man up!" or it is an expectation that men have the super power to be mentally strong no matter what challenege they face. We aren't all super human! 

For everyone of us there are improvements that we can make in our lives to improve our overall outlook and wellbeing. It isn't impossible to do. All you have to do is try!
